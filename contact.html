Bio

The process by which I make work centres around a physical research methodology involving paint, surface and figuration. I am interested in fragmenting space and reworking erasures of previous paintings by pulling on a space in time that is pushing to become something else. In playing with seemingly recognisable forms I am straddling the boundaries between abstraction and figuration in an attempt to create a sense of narrative to the surface.
