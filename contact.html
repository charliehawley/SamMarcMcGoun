Bio

The process by which I make work involves a physical research with both paint and surface and my own experience of meditated imagery.

I fragment and rework often seemingly recognisable scenes and refrain from using pure abstraction in an attempt to create ambiguity and bring a sense of narrative to the surface.

Rendering the paint and inserting motifs and symbols allows communication between both form and context.

I'm attempting to merge the gap between my drawing and paintings, combining the disposable nature and the physical weight of the canvas. To do this there is a constant researching of layers, which consistently challenge my eye to gauge when to reconstruct and when to deconstruct.
